A seasonal, retro 1950s drive-in at 801 W Park St in Livingston, Montana. Burgers, chili dogs, ice cream, and flavored malts. The kind of place where you eat in your car, the menu hasn't changed since the Eisenhower administration, and that's exactly how everyone wants it. Open seasonally, so plan accordingly or weep into your off-season salad.
