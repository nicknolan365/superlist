The Applebee's at 718 Hogan Rd in Bangor, Maine. 4.3 stars. 2,800 reviews. Not a single one of those reviewers expected their neighborhood grill and bar to end up on the SuperList, and yet here we are. Riblets, half-price apps, and the steadfast consistency of a chain restaurant in Stephen King's hometown. The 2-for-$25 is always calling.
