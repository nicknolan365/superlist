A coffee roaster in Boring, Oregon, that is anything but boring. Started when they began roasting beans outside the door of their landscaping supply business, Boring Bark. Customers started showing up more for the coffee than the mulch. Fair trade, small batch, genuinely great coffee from a town that insists on underselling itself.
