A Santa-themed gift shop at 101 St Nicholas Dr in North Pole, Alaska. Yes, the town is called North Pole. Yes, the street is called St Nicholas Drive. There's a giant Santa statue, a pastry shop, made-in-Alaska items, and you can send letters with a North Pole postmark. The commitment to the bit is year-round and absolute. Between this and the Santa Claus Post Office in Indiana, the SuperList is now the internet's premier directory of Santa-related destinations.
