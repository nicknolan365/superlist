It's a Goodwill on Summer Avenue in Memphis, Tennessee. The racks are packed, the dressing rooms have seen things, and there's a 50/50 chance you'll find a vintage Memphis Grizzlies jersey buried under a pile of khakis. The pricing is vibes-based. The parking lot is an experience. It's exactly what you'd expect and somehow more.
