A diner on Route 66 in Seligman, Arizona, with the tagline "You Kill It, We Grill It." The menu features items like "The Chicken That Didn't Cross the Road" and "Splatter Platter." The food is actually solid diner fare — burgers, fries, shakes — but the commitment to the roadkill bit is what gets people to pull over. Seligman is also widely credited as the inspiration for Radiator Springs in the Pixar film Cars.
