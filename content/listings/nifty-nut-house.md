A nut store at 537 N St Francis Ave in Wichita, Kansas, that has been a local tradition since 1937. Over 4,500 items across countless shelves — classic and modern candies, nuts, and snacks. The name "Nifty Nut House" has been making people smile for nearly 90 years. It's the kind of store that smells incredible the second you walk in and you leave with three pounds of cashews you didn't intend to buy.
