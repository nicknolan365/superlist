A 76,000 square foot roadside attraction at 510 Main St in Wall, South Dakota, that started in 1931 as a small drugstore and grew into one of the most aggressively advertised places in America. Signs for Wall Drug appear on every highway in the western half of the country and reportedly on every continent. They offer free ice water, which is what got people to stop in the first place. Over 2 million visitors a year in a town of 800 people.
