Home of the Forevertron — the second-largest scrap metal sculpture in the world — at S7703 US-12 in North Freedom, Wisconsin. It stands 50 feet high, stretches 120 feet wide, and weighs 300 tons. Built in the 1980s entirely from salvaged industrial parts. Dr. Evermor was not a doctor. His real name was Tom Every. But when you build a 300-ton sculpture out of scrap metal, you get to call yourself whatever you want.
