A 1907 Georgian Revival home at 511 9th St in Mena, Arkansas, built by a Kansas City Southern Railroad Conductor. You can also stay in The Fort Worth Cottage, built in 1944 as a wedding present for a bomber pilot returning from England. The cottage was a gift from his father, which is either the most generous or most controlling wedding present in history depending on your perspective.
