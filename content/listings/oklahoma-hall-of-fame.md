A museum at 1400 Classen Dr in Oklahoma City celebrating distinguished Oklahomans through high-tech, interactive displays. The state has produced Will Rogers, Mickey Mantle, Reba McEntire, and the inventor of the shopping cart. All of them are explored here. If you didn't know Oklahoma had a Hall of Fame, now you do, and the interactive displays are apparently very good.
