A brewery and taproom at 403 Hill Road in Greensboro Bend, Vermont — or as they call it, "the middle of somewhere." Consistently rated one of the best breweries in the world, tucked into Vermont's Northeast Kingdom where the nearest anything is a long drive away. The beer is worth the trip. The setting is worth the second trip. Bring a cooler.
