A waterfront village at 34 Harbor Blvd in Destin, Florida, featuring entertainment, shopping, dining, and boating excursions. It's the kind of place where you go for a seafood dinner and end up on a dolphin cruise you didn't plan for, holding a frozen daiquiri you don't remember ordering. Destin's answer to the question "what if a boardwalk had a personality?"
