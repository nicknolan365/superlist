A cookie and ice cream shop at 353 E 10th St in Gilroy, California. Cookies baked from scratch daily with quality ingredients, combined with creamy rich ice cream. Gilroy is known as the "Garlic Capital of the World," so the fact that someone opened a cookie shop there instead of doubling down on garlic is either brave or strategic. The cookies are excellent regardless.
