Omaha Metro's best outfitter on the Elkhorn River, at 25205 Harrison St in Waterloo, Nebraska. They go by "T&A" for short, which is either a bold branding decision or a completely innocent abbreviation that nobody on the team thought twice about. Either way, they provide a fun day on the river with a focus on safety and keeping the water clean. The name is doing a lot of unpaid marketing.
