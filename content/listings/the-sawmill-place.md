Classic Southern fare made with local ingredients in a log cabin at 1150 Pat Haralson Dr in Blairsville, Georgia. Breakfast and lunch only. The kind of place where the biscuits are the size of your fist and the gravy has its own gravitational pull. If you're looking for a trendy brunch spot with avocado toast, keep driving. This is real food in a real cabin.
