The World's Largest Reptile Zoo, per the Guinness Book of World Records, at 8955 US-16 near Rapid City, South Dakota. One of the largest collections of reptile species of any zoo on the planet, including some of the world's deadliest snakes. It's been a South Dakota destination for decades. If you're driving to Mount Rushmore, you're passing it, and the giant signs will make sure you know.
