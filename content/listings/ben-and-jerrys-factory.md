The Ben & Jerry's factory at 1281 Waterbury-Stowe Rd in Waterbury, Vermont, offers guided tours, free samples, and a literal Flavor Graveyard where discontinued ice cream flavors have headstones with epitaphs. It feels like a tourist trap. It is a tourist trap. But the ice cream is good and the graveyard is genuinely funny, so people keep showing up by the busload and leaving happy.
