A coffee and ice cream shop at 909 US-95 in Jordan Valley, Oregon, that advertises "Unforgettable Restrooms" — in quotes, on their website, like that. They are apparently much more than just a great coffee shop, and the restrooms are, by their own account, unforgettable. Jordan Valley has a population of about 180 people. The bar for unforgettable restrooms is whatever you want it to be out there.
