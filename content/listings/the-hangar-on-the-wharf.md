A restaurant at 2 Marine Way in Juneau, Alaska, built inside a converted airplane hangar on a wooden pier. Varied fare, 20+ beers on tap, large windows, and seaplanes that literally pull up outside while you eat. That's not a metaphor. Actual seaplanes taxi up to the restaurant. You're eating fish and chips while a floatplane docks ten feet away. There is no restaurant on the SuperList — or possibly the planet — with a cooler entrance.
