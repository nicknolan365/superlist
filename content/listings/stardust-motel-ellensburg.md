A small motel off I-90 in Ellensburg, Washington, with a neon sign that looks like it was designed during the first Bush administration and never touched again. The rooms are clean, the Wi-Fi works (usually), and the front desk closes at 10 PM sharp. It's the kind of place where the ice machine hums all night and checkout is on the honor system. Rates are suspiciously reasonable.
