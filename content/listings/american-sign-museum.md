A museum at 1330 Monmouth Ave in Cincinnati, Ohio, that preserves and displays a massive collection of signs — neon, porcelain, painted, illuminated, you name it. Also displays the equipment used to design and manufacture them. Opened in 2005 after Tod Swormstedt spent six years building the collection. It's a love letter to the golden age of American commercial signage, and it glows.
