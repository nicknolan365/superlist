In 1950, the host of the NBC game show "Truth or Consequences" promised to air an episode from the first town that renamed itself after the show. Hot Springs, New Mexico, took the deal. Over 70 years later, the name stuck and nobody changed it back. Locals call it "T or C" and the hot springs are still great.
