A 1950s-inspired family restaurant at 4201 Warden Rd in North Little Rock, Arkansas. The shakes are iconic, the vibes are nostalgic, and the domain name is "ilovepurplecow.com," which tells you everything about the energy here. A visit is less of a meal and more of a moment — laughter, nostalgia, and the realization that you've eaten an unreasonable amount of french fries.
