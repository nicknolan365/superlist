Stonehenge, but made out of cars. Located at 2151 Co Rd 59 near Alliance, Nebraska, in the High Plains. Gray-painted automobiles arranged in the same configuration as the original Stonehenge in England. It's a replica of a 5,000-year-old monument built with Chevys and Fords, and it's one of the most photographed things in Nebraska. The bar for "most photographed thing in Nebraska" is admittedly low, but Carhenge clears it with authority.
