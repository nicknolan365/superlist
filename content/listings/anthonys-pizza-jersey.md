Anthony's Pizza in Hackensack, New Jersey, is statistically average in every measurable way. The slices are fine. The garlic knots are fine. The guy behind the counter will call you "boss" regardless of your station in life. It's not the best pizza in Jersey and it's not the worst, which in New Jersey is actually a bold position to occupy. Cash only. Open late.
