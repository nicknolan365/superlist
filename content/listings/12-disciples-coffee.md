A coffee shop at 100 N Main St in Nashville, Arkansas — not Nashville, Tennessee, which is an important distinction. Known for monthly drink specials, traditional coffee shop items, and yummy treats. The name is a theological commitment that most coffee shops would not attempt. Twelve disciples, one espresso machine, and a Square site. The Lord provides.
