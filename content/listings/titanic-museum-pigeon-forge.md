A museum at 2134 Parkway in Pigeon Forge, Tennessee, that is literally shaped like the Titanic. Half-scale replica of the ship, filled with recovered artifacts from the actual wreck. Upon entry, each guest receives a boarding pass with the name of a real Titanic passenger. At the end, you find out if your person survived. It's educational, emotional, and located between a go-kart track and a pancake house. Peak Pigeon Forge.
