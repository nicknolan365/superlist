At 75,593 square feet, the Buc-ee's in Luling, Texas, is the largest convenience store on the planet. It has 120 gas pumps, a 255-foot car wash, and more beef jerky varieties than most people see in a lifetime. The brisket sandwiches are world-class. The bathrooms have won actual awards. It's a gas station that people plan road trips around, and that sentence is not a joke.
