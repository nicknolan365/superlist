Accident, Maryland, is a scenic small town in Garrett County. The name reportedly comes from a surveying mishap — two different parties accidentally claimed the same plot of land. The town leaned into it. It's actually a beautiful mountain community, but good luck telling anyone where you live without them asking if you're okay.
