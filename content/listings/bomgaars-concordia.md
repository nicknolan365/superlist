A family-owned farm and ranch supply store at 1620 Lincoln St in Concordia, Kansas. They sell everything: farm equipment, workwear, boots, hardware, automotive parts, power tools, lawn and garden equipment, and probably a few things you didn't know you needed until you walked in. It's the kind of store where you go in for fence posts and leave with a new pair of boots and a stock tank.
