One of the premier golf courses on the Outer Banks at 300 W Eckner St in Kitty Hawk, North Carolina. Open to the public year-round with club rentals and a fully stocked pro shop. The Sandtrap Tavern on-site serves breakfast and lunch with a full bar. You're golfing where the Wright Brothers first flew, which means your slice into the ocean is historically appropriate.
