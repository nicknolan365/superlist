Gorat's has been serving steaks in Omaha since 1944, but it's most famous for being Warren Buffett's favorite restaurant. The billionaire reportedly orders a T-bone, rare, with a double order of hash browns. The dining room looks like it hasn't been redecorated since the Nixon administration, and that's entirely the point. It's an Omaha institution.
