A lodging and event center at E0210 Rd & N0072 Rd in Boise City, Oklahoma. Twenty-two miles from any town, any grocery store, or any gas station. Scarce cell service. And yet, on a quiet evening looking across the Great Plains, you feel so close to Heaven. Their words, not ours — and they're not wrong. The address is literally two rural road intersections. "Make the middle of nowhere your destination" is the tagline, and they mean it literally.
