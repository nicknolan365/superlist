A one-room museum at 98775 CA-111 in Mecca, California, containing over 20,000 banana-themed items. In 1999, it set a Guinness World Record as the largest museum devoted to a single fruit — a category that apparently needed a record holder. It's in a town called Mecca, it's about bananas, and it's a world record holder. Every part of that sentence is real.
