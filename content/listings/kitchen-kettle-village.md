Over 40 local shops in one walkable village in the heart of Intercourse, Pennsylvania. Homemade jams, Amish quilts, handcrafted leather goods, and fresh shoofly pie. The name of the town gets all the attention, but the shopping keeps people coming back. Family-run since 1954.
