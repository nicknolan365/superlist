A winery and bistro at 412 W 2nd St in Roswell, New Mexico. They support local wineries and breweries across the state. Roswell is best known for aliens, but this place is proof that the town has more going on than extraterrestrial conspiracy theories. Stop by for a glass of wine and a bite to eat. No one will probe you.
