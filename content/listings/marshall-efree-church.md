An Evangelical Free Church at 410 Airport Rd in Marshall, Minnesota. Part of the Evangelical Free Church of America, committed to the proclamation of the Gospel and the authority of Scripture. It's on Airport Road, which in a town like Marshall means it's near a small regional runway, not LaGuardia. The kind of church where the parking lot is full on Sunday mornings and the potluck game is elite.
