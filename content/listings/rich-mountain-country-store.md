A country store at 1933 US-270 in Mena, Arkansas. Friendly folks who like to talk and share. Breakfast and burgers all day. Drinks and snacks all the time. Hikers, bikers, motorcyclists, tourists, and regulars who drive 30 miles daily are all welcome. Train watchers can use the porch. That last detail is the most Arkansas sentence ever written and we mean that as a compliment.
