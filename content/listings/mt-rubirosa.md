An outpost of the acclaimed Rubirosa Ristorante from NYC's Mulberry Street, now at 501 E Dean St in Aspen, Colorado. World-famous Tie-Dye Pizza, house-made pastas, legendary Sunday sauce, and a slopeside dining experience that transports you to the Italian Rockies. It's a New York institution in a ski town, which means the pizza is excellent and the prices are Aspen.
