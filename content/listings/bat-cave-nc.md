Bat Cave, North Carolina, is a real unincorporated community in Henderson County. Named after an actual bat cave in the nearby mountains. The mailing address is the best part — imagine putting "Bat Cave, NC" as your return address on every piece of mail you send. Bruce Wayne could never.
