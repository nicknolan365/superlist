American fare with global influences and a martini list, served amid brick walls, high ceilings, and abstract art at 124 N 3rd St in Grand Forks, North Dakota. It's the kind of restaurant that makes you forget you're in North Dakota, and then you step outside and remember immediately. The name is unexplained and delightful.
