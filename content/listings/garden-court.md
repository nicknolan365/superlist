A wedding venue at 1044 Alta Vista Rd in Louisville, Kentucky, tucked into the Schlegel Hall area of the Louisville Presbyterian Theological Seminary campus. It's the kind of venue where the photos look like they belong in a magazine, the gardens do all the heavy lifting for your decorator, and your aunt will absolutely cry during the ceremony. Louisville does weddings right.
