Nothing, Arizona is a ghost town on US Route 93. At its peak, it had a gas station and a few residents. The welcome sign once read: "The staunch citizens of Nothing are full of Hope, Faith, and Believe in the Future of Nothing." The gas station is closed. There is, quite literally, nothing here. And we listed it anyway.
