A Waffle House at 1851 Government St in Mobile, Alabama. All-day breakfast, signature waffles, and the kind of operational consistency that FEMA literally uses as a disaster severity metric (the "Waffle House Index"). This location is one of roughly 2,000 nationwide, and every single one of them has the same energy: fluorescent lights, a jukebox nobody uses, and a cook who can make your hash browns six ways without looking at the grill.
