A legendary waterfront bar and grill at 17401 Perdido Key Dr in Pensacola, Florida, sitting right on the Florida-Alabama state line. Open since 1964. Oysters, pub grub, and live music every single day. They host the annual Interstate Mullet Toss, where people throw dead fish across the state line. It's exactly as chaotic as it sounds and it's been going strong for 60 years. A Gulf Coast institution.
