A museum at 206 W Houston St in Floydada, Texas, dedicated entirely to Boston Terriers. The website appears to also sell puppies, which raises more questions than it answers. Floydada calls itself the "Pumpkin Capital of the USA," so between the pumpkins and the Boston Terrier museum, this town has fully committed to being niche. We respect it.
