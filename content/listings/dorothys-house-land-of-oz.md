A replica of Dorothy's house from The Wizard of Oz at 567 E Cedar St in Liberal, Kansas. Complete with an annual festival and enough Oz memorabilia to make you question whether Kansas has built its entire tourism strategy around a movie where the main character's only goal is to leave Kansas. The answer is yes, and it works.
