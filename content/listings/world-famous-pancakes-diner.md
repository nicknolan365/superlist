A small-town diner in Shelbyville, Indiana, that has claimed "World Famous Pancakes" on its sign for as long as anyone can remember. Are they world famous? Almost certainly not. Are they good? By most accounts, yes. The sign does more marketing than most agencies could dream of. The coffee is bottomless. The waitress calls you "hon." This is peak Americana.
