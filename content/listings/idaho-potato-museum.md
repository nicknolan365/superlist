A museum at 130 NW Main St in Blackfoot, Idaho, housed in a former train depot, dedicated to the history and cultivation of potatoes. There's a café and a gift shop. Idaho committed to being the potato state a long time ago and this museum is the logical conclusion of that commitment. The gift shop sells potato fudge. We don't know how it tastes and we're not sure we want to.
