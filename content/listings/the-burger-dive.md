A quirky burger joint with checkered floors at 114 N 27th St in Billings, Montana. Inventive burgers, hot dogs, and real milkshakes. In 2016, owner Brad Halsten won the World Food Championships with the "I'm Your Huckleberry" burger, making The Burger Dive the World Burger Champion. The name is a Montana reference, the title is legitimate, and the burgers back it up.
