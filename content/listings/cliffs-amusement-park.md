An amusement park at 4800 Osuna Rd NE in Albuquerque with rides, roller coasters, games, and a water park. Seasonal hours, local vibes, and the kind of place where your kid will beg you to go on the same ride eleven times in a row. It's not Six Flags and it doesn't need to be. It's Cliff's, and Albuquerque loves it.
