A long-standing cinema at 1000 Riverside Blvd in Norfolk, Nebraska. New releases, digital sound, stadium seating. It's seven screens of Hollywood in a town of 25,000 people. The kind of theater where the popcorn butter is real, the seats recline just enough, and you might see your high school English teacher three rows up watching the same Marvel movie.
