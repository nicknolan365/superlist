A chocolate company on the Big Island of Hawaii that sells chocolate-covered macadamia nuts called, yes, Donkey Balls. The "Dirty Balls" variety is coated in cocoa powder. The branding is ridiculous on purpose and it has worked spectacularly. Free factory tours available.
