An RV park at 98 2nd St on Highway 16 in the foothills of the Big Horn Mountains, just outside Ten Sleep, Wyoming. 52 full-service pull-through sites, sleeping cabins, bunkhouses, a tent area, hot showers, free Wi-Fi, pure artesian water, and — this is real — a Horse Hotel for travelers with horses. The town is called Ten Sleep because it was reportedly ten sleeps (days of travel) from several important Native American landmarks.
