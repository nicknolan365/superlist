The Taco Bell Cantina at 5200 CA-1 in Pacifica, California, sits on a cliff overlooking the Pacific Ocean and has been called the "World's Most Beautiful Taco Bell." 4.4 stars, 4,376 reviews. They serve alcohol. The sunset views are legitimately stunning. You're eating a Crunchwrap Supreme while watching waves crash against the California coast. This is the American dream operating at full capacity.
