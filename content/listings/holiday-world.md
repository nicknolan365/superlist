A full-sized theme park in Santa Claus, Indiana. Roller coasters, water slides, and free unlimited soft drinks — which is a genuinely aggressive perk. The park is themed around holidays: Christmas, Halloween, Thanksgiving, and the 4th of July. It's been open since 1946, making it one of the oldest theme parks in the country.
