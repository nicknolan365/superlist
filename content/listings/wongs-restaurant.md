A Chinese restaurant at 712 Opal Ave in Clinton, Oklahoma. No website, no description, no frills. Just Wong's, on Opal Avenue, in a town of 9,000 people on Route 66, serving Chinese food to western Oklahoma. Every small town in America has a Wong's or a Golden Dragon or a China Garden, and every single one of them is holding the community together in ways that don't get enough credit.
